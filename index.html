Help Center

Getting Started

Point your camera at a flat surface or a Hiro marker to activate AR content. Ensure good lighting for best results.

Hold your device steadily for more stable tracking.

Using the App

Tap the menu button (☰) to access AR options and customize your experience.

Each option can be expanded by tapping to see additional settings.

Tips for Best Experience

• Use in a well-lit environment

• Avoid reflective surfaces

• Keep your device steady when scanning

• Try different angles if tracking is unstable
