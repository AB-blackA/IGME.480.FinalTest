Using This App

Point your camera at a Hiro marker to see AR content. The 3D object will appear automatically when the marker is detected.

You can find Hiro markers online to print if you don't have one.

Marker Tips

• Keep the marker flat on a surface

• Ensure good lighting

• Hold your device steady

• Try different angles if needed
