Using This App

Point your camera at a Hiro marker to see AR content. The 3D object will appear automatically when the marker is detected.

When near a location, tap the screen to add its unique shape to your collection!

Location Features

The app will automatically check off clues when you reach specific locations in the cemetery.

Make sure to enable location services for full functionality.
